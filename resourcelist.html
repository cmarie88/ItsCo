by Robert Collins on unsplash

October 2019

20 links to guide you into web development:

My hope in sharing this list is that you have some life preservers, some direction while you're swimming in the sea of information! I hope you explore, and craft your own plan using some tools I offer here. This list includes what I plan to study, but since I have not taken all of these tutorials I offer them to you with the caveat that I cannot speak to their effectiveness quite yet. I have taken the HTML/CSS courses, and can confidently say they will give you an awesome foundation!
