Hey, I’m Casey, creator of /*It's Complicoded */

I’m a busy single parent of two rad boys, and I’m learning how to code! With a voracious appetite for learning, trying and creating, discovering coding has been like a beam of light shining brightly on my path ahead.

But why is it so complicoded you ask?

Well, amidst studying a million different resources to learn how to become a web developer, I’m also practicing the best version of motherhood that I can. Rooted in strong values, I put my sons on top of the priority list everyday, and am a firm believer that anything can be learned. On the side I am an aerialist, choreographer, hiker, and personal trainer. I also practice gratitude every day... As my hunky boyfriend says, I’m busy :)

These days, my main focus is learning to code...

The endgame I envision is a remote position in development, that pays a fair wage and offers benes to support me and my kiddos. They cost alot. That is no joke. My most valuable lessons so far: it is not a straight path, and embrace the fails , as they are teaching me to be better at learning.

I am a fairly excitable and emotional beast, but I’m striving for temperance, consistency and mastery .

This blog is a window into my life and learning, lovely catastrophe may she be.

Welcome! And Enjoy :)
